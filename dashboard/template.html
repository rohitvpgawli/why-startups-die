Every YC startup since 2021, and what became of it.

A living dataset built from the YC directory, the Wayback Machine, Hacker News, and SEC Form D filings — with LLM-extracted, evidence-linked shutdown reasons. Outcomes, not obituaries: the cohort includes everything from IPO to wind-down.

Read the rates carefully. Recent cohorts are right-censored — a 2025 company hasn't had time to reach an outcome. Low shutdown rates in young batches are unfinished observations, not survival.
